Our Story

Founded in 2010, PURNASATWA began as a small family farm with just 10 cows. Our passion for pure, organic dairy and commitment to traditional methods quickly gained the trust of local families.

Today, we've grown to serve over 10,000 families across the region, while staying true to our core values of purity, transparency and customer trust.

Every product that leaves our farm reflects our dedication to quality, freshness and your family's health.

Our Values

Purity

We never compromise on the purity of our products. Every item is 100% natural with no artificial additives.

Transparency
